About Us

Hutch Training Team

Jeff Leek - Chief Data Officer

jtleek@fredhutch.org

Jeff is Chief Data Officer, Vice President, and J Orin Edson Foundation Chair of Biostatistics at Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center. He leads the Fred Hutch Data Science Lab in building a data environment that is both cutting edge and friendly, supportive, open, and collaborative.

More about Jeff

Ted Laderas - Director of Training and Community

tladera2@fredhutch.org

Ted Laderas has worked with lots of different data types and knows his way around a workflow. He champions building learning communities of practice in science and research that are psychologically safe and inclusive. Ted leads the training and community efforts related to data science for Fred Hutch.

More about Ted

Chris Lo - Data Science Instructor

clo2@fredhutch.org

Chris Lo is a data science instructor who is passionate about helping biomedical and clinical researchers learn new tools to be effective at their research. He has a background in biostatistics and computational biology and has taught students of various backgrounds in biomedical data science

DaSL Affiliate Scientists

Carrie Wright - Senior Staff Scientist

cwright2@fredhutch.org

Carrie has a background in genomics and brain imaging analysis. Her current work is focused on innovating ways to make data science and computational biology more accessible. She is a member of the Open Case Studies team and the Informatics Technology for Cancer Research (ITCR) Training Network (ITN). She also currently leads the ITCR Operations, Promotion, Education, and Networking (OPEN) Group and the BioOCS project - which is an extension of the Open Case Studies Project.

More about Carrie

Ava Hoffman - Senior Staff Scientist

ahoffma2@fredhutch.org

Ava is a Senior Staff Scientist. She helps lead the AnVIL Project and the Genomic Data Science Community Network (GDSCN), exploring ways to make genomics research more accessible by connecting communities to cloud-based resources. Previously, she researched ecological genomics and worked as a data scientist in industry.

More about Ava

Elizabeth Humphries - Senior Staff Scientist

ehumphri@fredhutch.org

Elizabeth did her PhD in molecular epidemiology and the genetics of mood disorders, and her MS in evolutionary and population genetics in birds. She works on the AnVIL Project.

More about Elizabeth

Kate Isaacs - Postdoc

kisaac@fredhutch.org

Kate has a background in functional and evolutionary genomics where she built simulation and machine learning models. She now works as part of the [Informatics Technology for Cancer Research (ITCR) Training Network (ITN)](https://www.itcrtraining.org/), [AnVIL Project](https://anvilproject.org/), and [Open Case Studies](https://www.opencasestudies.org/) teams.

More about Kate

Oshane Thomas - Postdoc

othomas@fredhutch.org

Oshane joined Fred Hutch as a postdoctoral fellow after completing his PhD in Biological Anthropology at the University of Illinois. Before this, he was at Seattle Children’s Research Institute and The Imageomics Institute, where he developed AI and geometry-based methods for automated 3D phenotyping. His work combines deep learning, geometric morphometrics, and open-source development in 3D Slicer (especially through SlicerMorph). Lately, he's been focused on shape correspondence learning, morphospace visualization, and AI-driven approaches to link genotype and phenotype. He currently works on the BioOCS project.

More about Oshane
