## Genomics Workshop

We are planning to offer a genomics workshop via Data Carpentry on July 20-21. This workshop will cover:

- [Project Organization and Management](https://datacarpentry.github.io/organization-genomics/)
- [Introduction to the Command Line](https://datacarpentry.github.io/shell-genomics/)
- [Genomics Data Wrangling and Processing](https://datacarpentry.github.io/wrangling-genomics/) (including QC workflows such as MultiQC)

Please express your interest here: <https://forms.gle/mrG6kG6J4H1MDycT6> - We will follow up via email with a more detailed registration form. We will use this information to better help you during the workshop.

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. **Is the workshop online or in-person?** Based on feedback, we have decided to make it online-only. 
2. **Is the workshop appropriate for beginners?** Yes, it is very beginner friendly and the only requirements are some familiarity with genomics. 
3. **What do I need to participate?** You will need a laptop and to follow the [setup instructions](https://datacarpentry.github.io/genomics-workshop/index.html#setup). You do not need to have Fred Hutch Cluster access.
4. **What is the schedule for the workshop?** It will roughly be from 9-5 PM on July 20-21 with a 1 hour break for lunch. Project and Data management will be morning of the 20th and  Intro to Command Line will be the afternoon of the 20th and morning of the 21st, and we will end with the Genomics Data Wrangling the afternoon of the 21st. 
5. **Does the workshop cost anything?** It is free. This workshop is sponsored by the Office of the Chief Data Officer.  As long as you are part of the Fred Hutch Consortium, it is free to you.
6. **Do I have to attend the entire workshop?** The workshop is a single unit and though the lessons are separate, they do build on each other. We highly recommend that you attend the entire workshop.
7. **Who is Data Carpentry?** [Data Carpentry](https://datacarpentry.org/) is part of [The Carpentries](https://carpentries.org/), which is an organization that brings training in necessary data skills for various fields and their data. We are currently a member of the Carpentries, which means that we can offer you with this workshop.
8. More [frequently asked questions](https://datacarpentry.github.io/genomics-workshop/faq) are available via Data Carpentry
